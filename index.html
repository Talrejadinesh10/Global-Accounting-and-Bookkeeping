About Our Firm

We are a globally-focused accounting and compliance firm that partners with CPA firms, business owners, and growing enterprises across the U.S., UK, Australia, and beyond. Our services are built to help you scale — whether you're seeking a reliable offshore team to support your accounting practice, or a business owner aiming to streamline your financial operations. From audit-ready bookkeeping to tax compliance and virtual CFO support, we deliver clarity, efficiency, and peace of mind. Our clients trust us for timely, accurate, and professional finance solutions tailored to cross-border needs.

Meet the Founder

I am Dinesh Talreja, a Chartered Accountant with deep experience in global audits, financial reporting, and systems implementation. Having worked with international audit firms and supported finance teams across the U.S., India, and other regions, I've seen firsthand how complex and fragmented financial operations can hinder business growth.
