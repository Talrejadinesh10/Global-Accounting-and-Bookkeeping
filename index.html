Our Core Services

Global Accounting Solutions

“Global Accounting Solutions” includes full end-to-end offshore finance support, from audit-ready bookkeeping and reconciliation to monthly and year‑end financial reporting. We work with platforms like QuickBooks, Xero, and NetSuite and ensure compliance with U.S. GAAP and IFRS frameworks. Clients benefit from streamlined workflows, reliable data accuracy, and centralized financial oversight. Our services also include cloud migration support, internal control setup, and performance dashboards for better visibility and decision-making.

India Tax & Compliance

“India Tax & Compliance” covers complete statutory compliance for Indian businesses — from GST registration and filing (GSTR‑1, GSTR‑3B) to TDS and Income Tax returns. We also handle payroll deductions, statutory employee compliance (PF/ESI), and ROC filings (AOC‑4, MGT‑7), ensuring timely and penalty-free submissions. Our team integrates compliance tasks into a single dashboard, so you stay informed and audit‑ready at every step. Ideal for startups and SMEs seeking total regulatory assurance with minimal effort.
