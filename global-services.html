Overview

We provide global accounting services designed for scalability, compliance, and operational efficiency. Whether you're a CPA firm or a multinational startup, our financial experts deliver cost-effective, audit-ready results tailored to your region and industry.

Services for CPA Firms

Accountant

Experienced CPAs delivering audit-ready financials, maintaining compliance, and helping CPA firms scale client operations with offshore staffing efficiency.

Bookkeeper

Daily data entry, bank reconciliations, and general ledger upkeep with tools like QuickBooks, Xero, and NetSuite — ensuring clean books for tax time.

Fractional CFO

Strategic insight across budgeting, forecasting, performance dashboards, and financial storytelling for clients needing executive-level finance direction.

Preparation of 1040s, 1120s, and other IRS forms, including schedules and depreciation — using ProSeries, UltraTax, or Drake for accurate submissions.

Staff Accountant

Manage journal entries, trial balances, month-end closing, fixed asset tracking, and GL cleanups with minimal supervision and top-tier quality.

Build and maintain PBC lists, tie-outs, confirmations, and support documents required by U.S. audit teams — trained in PCAOB & AICPA standards.

Certified QBO professionals for setup, data migration, automation, custom chart of accounts, and accurate report extraction with zero redundancy.

Tax Reviewer

Second-level QC reviews, deductions, audit flags, and last-mile validation before e-filing — enhancing CPA trust and reducing rework.
