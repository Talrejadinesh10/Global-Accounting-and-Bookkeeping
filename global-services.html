Offshore Audit Support

Assist CPA firms with audit documentation, work paper preparation, confirmations, and risk assessments. We follow U.S. GAAP and PCAOB guidelines ensuring readiness for annual audits.

Bookkeeping for U.S./UK Entities

Daily and monthly bookkeeping using QuickBooks, Xero, and NetSuite for U.S., UK, and Canadian companies. We manage accounts receivable, payable, and bank reconciliations.

Payroll Processing

End-to-end payroll services including pay computation, tax withholdings, and pay stub delivery in compliance with U.S. labor laws and timelines. Integration with Gusto, ADP, and other platforms.

Management Reporting

Monthly and quarterly performance dashboards, P&L summaries, and KPIs tailored to client needs. We offer insights that support financial planning and investor reporting.

Accounting System Migration

Migration support from legacy accounting systems to cloud platforms like Xero and QuickBooks Online. We ensure data integrity and minimal disruption during the transition.

Year-End Finalization

Preparation of year-end financials including journal adjustments, depreciation schedules, and closing entries. We work closely with external auditors to ensure smooth sign-off.
