Solutions for Global Clients

We partner with global small businesses and CPA firms to deliver dependable, audit-ready financial operations. Our team brings deep expertise in U.S. GAAP, IFRS, and global accounting tools like QuickBooks and Xero — ensuring accuracy, compliance, and transparency at every step. With a strong focus on process excellence and consistent delivery, we serve as an extension of your in-house finance team.

Services for CPA Firms

Accountant

Reliable support for general ledger, reconciliations, and financial reporting. Familiar with GAAP/IFRS. Helps CPA firms meet deadlines and stay compliant.

Bookkeeper

Handles daily entries, reconciliations, and cleanup. Works with QuickBooks/Xero. Ensures your books are always ready for tax or audit.

Fractional CFO

Strategic part-time financial leadership. Assists with budgeting, forecasting, and investor reporting. Ideal for growing firms.

Tax Preparer

Preparation of U.S. tax returns including 1040, 1120, and 1065. Uses leading software like UltraTax and Drake. Ensures timely and accurate filings.

Staff Accountant

Supports month-end close, journal entries, and accruals. Deep understanding of U.S. accounting standards. Reliable for ongoing operations.

Audit Support Staff

Provides assistance for U.S. audits: PBC prep, confirmations, and tie-outs. Trained in AICPA/PCAOB compliance.

QuickBooks Expert

QBO setup, data cleanup, and custom reports. Automates workflows and supports client file reviews for CPA practices.

Tax Reviewer

Final review of returns for accuracy, savings, and compliance. Reduces error rates and flags issues before filing.
