Bookkeeping Expert

We provide bookkeeping support that goes beyond transaction entry — including expense tracking, vendor bill management, bank reconciliations, and cash flow oversight. Our team ensures records are audit-ready and compliant with regulatory standards, helping CPA firms deliver accurate and timely reporting to their clients.

Staff Accountant

We provide staff accountants who manage day-to-day accounting records, payroll processing, and reconciliations while assisting with the preparation of draft financial statements. With a strong focus on compliance, our team ensures that all accounting outputs meet regulatory and client requirements.

Senior Accountant

Our senior accountants provide advanced support to CPA firms by reviewing staff accountants’ work, performing reconciliations, and preparing and finalizing financial statements. They handle complex accounting areas such as accruals, deferred taxes, and consolidations while ensuring full compliance with U.S. GAAP and regulatory standards. In addition, our team assists with audit preparation, variance analysis, and client-facing reporting — delivering accurate, high-quality outputs that strengthen your firm’s practice.

Tax Preparer

Our tax preparers support CPA firms with comprehensive tax planning and preparation, covering business tax filings, sales tax compliance, and multi-state tax management. We assist in identifying tax credits, deductions, and incentives while ensuring clients’ returns are accurate, timely, and compliant. From corporate returns to personal tax savings strategies, our team strengthens your firm’s ability to deliver value-driven tax solutions.

Audit Support Staff

We provide dedicated audit support staff to help CPA firms manage routine but critical tasks such as documentation, sampling, walkthroughs, lead schedule preparation, and control testing. Whether during peak season or for ongoing needs, our offshore team follows your firm’s SOPs to deliver accurate, compliant, and timely working papers — freeing your in-house team to focus on high-value client work.

AR/AP Billing Specialist

Our AR/AP billing specialists manage receivables and payables end-to-end, ensuring accurate tracking and timely settlements for your clients. We oversee accounts payable and accounts receivable, handle financial reconciliations, and support cash flow management to maintain liquidity. In addition, our team assists with risk management and provides tax and compliance support — helping CPA firms deliver smooth, reliable financial operations for their clients.

Virtual CFO

Our Virtual CFOs provide strategic financial leadership by supervising client accounts and records. We create detailed financial reports, manage cash flow, and develop budgets that align with business goals. In addition, we analyze market trends to offer data-driven strategic advice, helping CPA firms and their clients make informed decisions and maintain financial health.
