What We Offer in India

Our India division delivers complete compliance support for startups, SMEs, and private limited companies. We handle GST and TDS filings, income tax returns, payroll management, and ROC/MCA obligations — all with timely execution and zero penalty focus. Whether you need full-time bookkeeping or expert guidance for regulatory filings, we ensure your business stays compliant and audit-ready, always.

Our Core India Services

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Maintain clean ledgers, track expenses, and reconcile accounts using Tally, Zoho, or QuickBooks. Get audit-ready books for investors or statutory filing.

GST & TDS Return Filing

Monthly/quarterly GST returns, TDS compliance, and 26Q filing — with reconciliations to prevent mismatches and penalties.

Income Tax Return Filing

Prepare and file returns for individuals, firms, LLPs, and companies. Includes advance tax calculations and planning guidance.

ROC & MCA Filings

Annual filings like AOC-4, MGT-7, DIR-3 KYC, board resolutions, and more — all handled with reminders and complete documentation.

Payroll & TDS Computation

Generate payslips, deduct PF/ESI, calculate TDS, and file returns. Supports Gusto, Zoho Payroll, and custom Excel solutions.

MIS & Financial Dashboards

Monthly profit & loss, cash flow statements, KPI dashboards — insights that help business owners make fast, confident decisions.
